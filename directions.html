 FROM THE NORTH


  1. Take I-287 South.
  2. Merge onto US-202 S via exit number 17 toward US-22 W/ SOMERVILLE/ FLEMINGTON.
  3. Turn right onto Church St. (big intersection), just before the Flemington circle. Go through one light, then take the next right onto Main St.
  4. Go approximately .5 miles.
  5. Shaker Cafe is located on the left, next to the Post Office, near the corner of Capner St.



FROM THE SOUTH



  1. Take Rt. 31 North.
  2. After it merges with Rt. 202 (north of Pennington), continue on 202/31 North for another 5-10 min.
  3. You'll pass a bunch of car dealerships on both sides of the road; after the movie theater and Home Depot, turn right into a jug-handle and cross over 202/31.
  4. Turn right and go up South Main St. into another traffic circle.
  5. Take the 2nd spoke, staying on Main St. (keep the small shopping center on your left).
  6. Go through the light at Church St., and into town.
  7. The Shaker Cafe will be on your left, just before the Post Office, near the corner of Capner St.




FROM THE EAST



  1. Take Amwell Rd. (Rt. 514) west, past Neshanic.
  2. Turn right onto Clover Hill Rd. and go to the end (about 2 miles).
  3. Turn right onto Old York Rd. (Rt. 613), then the first left onto Voorhees Corner Rd. (Rt. 650).
  4. Cross over Rt. 202, where the road becomes Church St.
  5. At the second light, turn right onto Main St. and go into town.
  6. The Shaker Cafe will be on your left, just before the Post Office, near the corner of Capner St.




FROM THE WEST



  1. Take Rt. 12 from Frenchtown to the first traffic circle in Flemington, just after the light at Old Croton Rd.
  2. Take the 3rd spoke off the circle, onto Mine St. (a farm and a church will be on your left).
  3. Take Mine St. about a mile to the end, then turn left onto Main St.
  4. The Shaker Cafe will be a few blocks down on your left, just before the Post Office, near the corner of Capner St.
